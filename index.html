Another Life is the fifth mission in HITMAN 2. Agent 47 enters the quiet suburbia of Whittleton Creek, Vermont, USA, in order to ascertain clues to tracking down The Constant in his predecessor Janus' home and surrounding neighbourhood. He must also cover his tracks by eliminating Janus and the head of his security detail, Nolan Cassidy.
